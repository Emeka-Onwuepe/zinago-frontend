Principal Consultant | Legal & Regulatory Advisory

Dr. Nancy Nzom is a Nigerian legal practitioner, academic, consultant and Notary Public of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. She is the Principal Consultant at Zinago Law Practice, where she provides leadership to an advisory-led legal and HR practice delivering structured legal solutions across employment and labour law, immigration law, corporate and regulatory compliance, property and real estate law, family law, workplace governance, dispute resolution and related advisory services.

Her work supports individuals, employers, organisations and institutions navigating complex regulatory, workforce, property, family and compliance matters through clear, strategic and legally sound guidance.

Leadership & Advisory Orientation

At Zinago Law Practice, Dr. Nzom provides strategic leadership to a firm positioned around preventive legal guidance, regulatory compliance and structured decision-making. Her approach emphasises early-stage legal risk assessment, compliance sequencing and advisory clarity, enabling clients to make informed decisions across employment, investment, property, family and regulatory matters.

This leadership orientation ensures that the firm’s services are delivered with foresight, consistency and alignment to real-world institutional, commercial and personal contexts.

Academic & Professional Background

Dr. Nzom holds a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Employment Law, a Master of Laws (LL.M – General), and a Master of Science (MSc) in Industrial and Employment Relations.

She is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) and holds professional memberships with the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD), United Kingdom; the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria (CIPM); and the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (CIArb), United Kingdom.

This multidisciplinary background informs the firm’s ability to approach legal matters holistically, combining doctrinal legal analysis with practical regulatory, workplace, transactional and family-law insight.

Practice Philosophy
